About the App

Pixel Studio PRO is a powerful pixel art editor for professionals and enthusiasts alike. Create animations, sprites, and illustrations right from your phone or tablet.
Perfect for game designers, digital artists, and NFT creators.

advantages

  • Multi-layer animation with onion skin

  • Smart tools: fill, selection, symmetry

  • Export to .GIF, .PNG, .ASE and more

  • Intuitive UX optimized for touchscreens

  • Cloud saving and cross-platform sync

  • No ads, no distractions — just pure creativity
